Night

Essential Question: How do our experiences and the stories of people like Elie Wiesel help shape our identity and humanity? "A memoir of survival that challenges us to bear witness to the past to protect our future."

Unit Learning Purpose

Reading Writing Speaking Listening
Critical-Thinking: I can analyze evidence to understand the changes in Elie's character. Character & Creativity: I can create a visual representation to show how Wiesel’s story impacts my own identity. Communication: I can contribute to shared reading by using my voice with clarity and showing the courage to learn and pronounce new words. Collaboration: I can actively listen to others' ideas and build our collective understanding of Elie's experience.

Shaping Our Humanity

Essential Lesson: We are not just readers; we are witnesses. When we learn about Elie Wiesel's journey, we take a piece of his story and add it to our own identity.

"To listen to a witness is to become a witness." — Elie Wiesel
