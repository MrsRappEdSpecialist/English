Part 3: Written Response (CER)

Mini-Lesson: What is Imagery?
Imagery is descriptive language that appeals to our five senses (sight, sound, smell, taste, touch). Authors use imagery to paint a vivid picture in the reader's mind, creating a deeply immersive experience and evoking strong emotions.

Prompt: How does Elie Wiesel use imagery to convey the horrific reality of arriving at the camp?
