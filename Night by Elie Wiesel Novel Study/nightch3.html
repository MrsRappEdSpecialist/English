🎭 Module 4: How People Change

  • Eliezer: He goes "numb." His feelings shut down so he can survive. When his father is hit, Eliezer is too scared to help. He feels guilty, but survival takes over.
  • Bela Katz: A strong prisoner forced to work in the crematorium. He is forced to put his own father's body in the furnace. This shows the unimaginable cruelty of the camp.
🕯️ Central Theme Check-In: The Death of Faith
Disillusionment means losing your belief in something. Eliezer watches men pray to God, but he thinks it is absurd that God would let this happen. His faith is fading.
