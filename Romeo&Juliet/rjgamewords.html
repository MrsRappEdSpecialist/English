Word Fued

How to Play
1.Pick a Category 2. Roll a letter. 3. Write down as many words starting with that letter as you can.
Scoring
The Double-Cross: If two teams write the same word, it’s a "clash." Both teams lose the point for that word.
The Family Secret: If a team comes up with a word so obscure that no one else (and not even the teacher) thought of it, they get 3 points instead of 1.
The Exile Rule: If a team leaves a category blank, they are "Exiled" for that round and lose an additional point. Think fast!
